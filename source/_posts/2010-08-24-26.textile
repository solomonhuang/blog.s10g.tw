適用於系統上已有一套wine或是要同時測試不同版本的wine。
{% codeblock %}#!/usr/bin/env bash
WINEDIR=/full/path/of/your/wine
PATH=${WINEDIR}/bin:${PATH}

WINEDEBUG=-all
WINESERVER=${WINEDIR}/bin/wineserver
WINELOADER=${WINEDIR}/bin/wine
WINEDLLPATH=${WINEDIR}/lib/wine
export WINESERVER WINELOADER WINEDLLPATH WINEDEBUG PATH{% endcodeblock %}
<ul>
	<li>WINEDIR: WINE所安裝的目錄。</li>
	<li>WINESERVER: wineserver執行檔所在的位置。</li>
	<li>WINELOADER: 就是wine執行檔的位置。</li>
	<li>WINEDLLPATH: 指定wine提供的dll目錄。</li>
	<li>WINEDEBUG: -all把全部的除錯訊息關掉。</li>
	<li>把WINE的執行檔目錄加到PATH的第一個，才不會用到系統原有的。</li>
</ul>
